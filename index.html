Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Broadway Education Hub is committed to protecting the privacy of students and parents. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the information provided through our admission forms.

2. Information We Collect

When you fill out our admission form, we may collect the following information:

  • Student Name
  • Parent/Guardian Name
  • Phone Number
  • Class / Course Details
  • Other relevant admission details

3. Use of Phone Numbers

The phone numbers collected through the admission form will be used strictly for official communication purposes. Specifically:

  • To send admission confirmation details
  • To share admission-related updates
  • To send examination results
  • To communicate important academic information

These communications may be sent via WhatsApp or direct phone contact.

4. Data Protection

We take reasonable technical and administrative measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

5. Data Sharing

Broadway Education Hub does not sell, trade, or rent personal information to third parties. Your phone number and admission data will only be used for internal communication purposes.

6. Consent

By submitting the admission form, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy, including receiving messages via WhatsApp regarding admission and results.
